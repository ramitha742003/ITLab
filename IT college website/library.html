Library Overview

BMS College for Women Library has substantial information and it is enlightening as a knowledge centre. Through our resources, we are earnestly committed to enhance the quality of learning which has continuous services in our college. Our college library is housed on the first floor in a very spacious area and well seating capacity, with a separate reading room and a Reference section, Periodical section, Reprographic section, Competitive Book Corner, Technical section, Display of New Arrivals and so on.The library serves as a central information centre for students, staff and researchers. The Library offers an expansive collection comprising more than sixty thousands of Reference books, Textbooks, Journals (both National and International), periodicals and Other resources.
